About Me

Hey, I’m Siria and I’m a college student who also creates content on social media. I share my daily life on campus, study sessions, and realistic “what I actually eat” kinds of meals that you can make on a student budget.

I started this website as a place where I could go a bit deeper than a short video or Instagram caption. Here you’ll find study tips, product recommendations, and simple recipes that fit into a busy student schedule.

What You’ll Find Here

  • Study essentials: tech, stationery and small things that make revision less painful.
  • Budget cooking: easy recipes and basic tools that work in tiny student kitchens.
  • Realistic routines: ideas for organising your time without pretending everything is perfect.

How I Choose Products

Whenever I talk about a product, it’s either something I already use or something I’ve researched for myself as a student. If there are affiliate links on a page, it means I may earn a small commission if you decide to buy through those links. This helps support my content and the time I spend testing and reviewing things, but it never changes the price for you.
